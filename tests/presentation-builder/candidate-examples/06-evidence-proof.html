Key point: The August outage hurt Reliability but the business absorbed the impact — Revenue, Retention, Activation, and Expansion all held or exceeded targets, which tells us our customer relationships are resilient and our value proposition is stronger than the incident.

If challenged: The Reliability miss is real — 87% of target means we hit 99.2% uptime against a 99.9% OKR. That's 5.5 additional hours of downtime. The post-mortem is in Appendix D and the remediation is already live in the stack we'll cover next.

Transition: On Revenue specifically — 112% of target is the best quarter since launch, and it happened during a month when the product was partially down. That durability is worth unpacking, and it connects directly to the deployment improvements we made in September.
