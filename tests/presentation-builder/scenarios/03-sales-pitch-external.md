## Source Material

RESEARCH OUTPUT
Question: What is the current state of enterprise document processing, the pain points organizations face, and how does AI-powered intelligent document processing (IDP) transform outcomes?

RESEARCH BRIEF
- Enterprises process an average of 1.2 billion documents annually, with 80% containing unstructured data that resists traditional automation (Forrester 2024)
- Manual document processing costs enterprises $12-15 per document when accounting for labor, errors, and rework (McKinsey Operations Practice 2024)
- AI-powered IDP reduces processing cost per document to $0.50-2.00, a 85-95% reduction (Everest Group IDP PEAK Matrix 2025)
- Organizations deploying IDP report 73% faster processing times and 94% extraction accuracy, up from 60-70% with legacy OCR (ABBYY Industry Report 2024)
- The IDP market is projected to grow from $2.1B in 2024 to $11.8B by 2029, a 41% CAGR (MarketsAndMarkets 2024)
- Regulatory compliance failures related to document processing errors cost financial services firms an average of $14.8M annually in fines (Thomson Reuters Regulatory Intelligence 2024)
- 68% of enterprises report that document processing bottlenecks are the primary cause of customer onboarding delays exceeding 5 business days (Capgemini Digital Transformation Survey 2024)

EVIDENCE GAPS
- Long-term (5+ year) maintenance and retraining costs for IDP systems are not well documented in independent studies
- Comparative failure rates across different IDP vendors are not publicly available — most accuracy claims are vendor-reported
- Impact on workforce displacement and organizational change management outcomes is poorly studied beyond initial deployment phase
